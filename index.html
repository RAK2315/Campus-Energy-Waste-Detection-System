The Problem

Campuses are flying blind on energy.

One bill at the end of the month. No idea which building, which hour, which system is responsible.

  • Labs running at 3AM with nobody inside — nobody notices until the bill arrives
  • AC cooling empty buildings all weekend — treated as normal operating cost
  • Equipment peaks happening simultaneously — no load management whatsoever
  • Enterprise monitoring tools cost lakhs + require hardware — out of reach for most campuses

Under The Hood

Two models.
One pipeline.

Not rule-based thresholds. Not Excel formulas. Actual machine learning that adapts to your campus's own consumption patterns.

Anomaly Detection
Isolation Forest
Learns what "normal" looks like for your campus specifically. Automatically retrains on every dataset you upload — so a hostel block and a lab block are judged by their own baselines, not each other's.
